About

Helping you to make sense of your digital analytics data.

After more than 8 years working as a Digital Analyst, I have decided to consult on a freelance basis.

I work with a number of clients around the globe, offering support and niche skills that are often too specific to hire in-house. By working directly with clients and agencies, I am able to offer the Analytics services that you require on an independent, ad-hoc basis. I specialise in the technical aspects of Digital Analytics and have in-depth JavaScript experience. I’ve also helped out a number of clients with eCommerce tracking that requires dataLayer setups or server side implementation.

While I have worked and lived in Cape Town for most of my life, I recently spent a year travelling through Australia. I have also spent time travelling and working in Switzerland and Germany in the past.

When I am not working, you are likely to find me in the ocean or running on the mountains in and around Cape Town, South Africa.
